Our Mission: At PashuCare, our mission is to rescue animals in distress, provide essential veterinary care, and facilitate their safe rehabilitation. We work tirelessly to ensure every animal experiences kindness, protection, and the chance to thrive in a loving environment.

Our Vision: We envision a future where animals and humans live in mutual respect and harmony. Our goal is to restore the natural bond between all living beings and the Earth—creating a sustainable, empathetic, and cruelty-free world where animals are valued as sentient beings.

Our Approach: Through compassionate rescue, high-quality medical treatment, community adoption programs, and public education, we take a comprehensive approach to animal welfare. By empowering people and building infrastructure for long-term change, we ensure lasting impact in animal care.
